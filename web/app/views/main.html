Trace a contributor

Want to know how you are connected to another PHP fanatic on GitHub? With the trace tool you can find the shortest path (in terms of repositories related users collaborate on) between any two users who collaborate on PHP packages accessible through Packagist and hosted on Github. Are we really all separated by six degrees? Now in the world of Packagist you can find out.

Find a new contributor

It can be hard to find new collaboarators. Enter the name of a Package and you will be provided with a prioritised list of potential future collaborators. This list is generated such that existing collaborators have strong social ties with new potentials. Turbo charge your recruitment process for new or ailing projects!
